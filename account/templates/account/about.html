About Us

Join us in our journey to redefine beauty and inspire a generation to embrace their uniqueness with confidence and pride. Together, let’s go “beyond the filter” and celebrate the beauty of authenticity.

A project initiated by a passionate student at the National University of Modern Languages (Islamabad).

This is an initiative born out of a passion for promoting self-love and body positivity in today’s digital age.

Driven by my own experiences and the realization of the adverse effects of excessive social media use on mental health, I embarked on a mission to raise awareness about the issue.

Through “Beyond the Filter,” my goal is to empower individuals to embrace their true selves, free from the pressures of superficial beauty ideals.
